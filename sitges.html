Sitges

Sitges is a coastal town in Spain's Catalonia region, southwest of Barcelona, backed by the mountainous Parc Natural del Garraf. It's known for its Mediterranean beaches and seafront promenade lined with grand mansions. The compact old town and surrounding streets are filled with shops, restaurants. The Maricel Museum and Cau Ferrat Museum showcase Catalan and other Spanish art. Population is 28,478.
